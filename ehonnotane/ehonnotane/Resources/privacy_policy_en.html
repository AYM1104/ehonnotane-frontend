Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

ehonnotane ("the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

The App may collect the following information:

1.1 Account Information

  • Email address (via authentication providers)
  • Display name / Nickname
  • Profile picture (optional)
  • User ID provided by authentication providers

1.2 Child Information

  • Child's nickname
  • Date of birth (to customize storybook content appropriately)
  • Gender (optional)

1.3 Uploaded Images

  • Images uploaded for storybook generation

1.4 Usage Information

  • App usage patterns
  • Generated storybook history
  • Credit usage history
  • Purchase history

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

Information Type Purpose
Account Information User authentication, account management, customer support
Child Information Generating age-appropriate storybook content
Uploaded Images Storybook story and image generation (AI processing)
Usage Information Service improvement, statistical analysis, billing management

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

3.1 Authentication Services

  • Auth0: For user authentication
  • Apple: Sign in with Apple
  • Google: Google Account sign-in
  • LINE: LINE Account sign-in
  • Twitter/X: Twitter Account sign-in

3.2 AI and Cloud Services

  • Google Cloud (Gemini API): For story and image generation; uploaded images and prompts are sent to this service
  • Google Cloud Storage: Image storage
  • Supabase: Database management

3.3 Payment Services

  • Apple App Store: Subscription payment processing (payment information is not stored by the App)

4. Data Retention

Information Type Retention Period
Account Information Until account deletion
Uploaded Images Deleted from servers within 30 days after storybook generation
Generated Storybooks Until account deletion
Purchase History As required by applicable laws

5. Children's Privacy

The App is a service for creating children's storybooks. Child information (nickname, date of birth) is used solely to customize storybook content appropriately and is not shared with third parties or used for advertising purposes.

Children under 13 should use the App under parental consent and supervision.

6. Data Security

We implement the following security measures to protect user information:

  • SSL/TLS encrypted communications
  • Access control and authentication
  • Regular security updates

11. Region-Specific Disclosures

11.1 For European Economic Area (EEA) Users – GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the following additional rights and disclosures apply:

  • Legal Basis for Processing: We process your personal data based on:
    • Your consent (for account creation and service usage)
    • Performance of a contract (to provide our services)
    • Legitimate interests (to improve our services and prevent fraud)
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting us or deleting your account.
  • Right to Lodge Complaint: You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.
  • Data Transfers: Your data may be transferred to servers located outside the EEA. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

11.2 For California Residents – CCPA

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Know: You may request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected.
  • Right to Delete: You may request deletion of your personal information.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.
  • Do Not Sell: We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@ehonnotane.com.

11.3 For Users in the United States – COPPA

The Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) requires parental consent for the collection of personal information from children under 13.

  • Our service is intended for use by parents/guardians to create storybooks for their children.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13.
  • Child information (nickname, date of birth) is provided by the parent/guardian.
  • Parents may review, delete, or refuse further collection of their child's information by contacting us.
